BHARAT BOOK AI - PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: May 2026

At Bharat Book AI, we believe in radical transparency and strict data sovereignty. This privacy policy describes how we collect, store, and safeguard your corporate financial and operational records.

1. NO COOKIE HARVESTING & RADICAL PRIVACY
------------------------------------------------------
Bharat Book AI is structurally designed around local storage and client-first execution. 
* We do not sell, rent, or lease your transaction histories to any third party.
* Your accounting books, tax records, ledgers, and raw document scans remain strictly in your device's storage unless you explicit instruct the app to sync with a secure customer-provisioned hosting instance.

2. SPECIFIC DATA WE PROCESS
------------------------------------------------------
* Company Directory & Profiles: Provided by your administrator to provision authorization flags (Auditor, Accountant, Viewer).
* System Diagnostics & Tickets: Explicitly initiated by you. When exporting a telemetry log or submitting a support ticket, only the required fields you verify are sent to our Indian security team.
* AI Analysis Insights: Local processing ensures no sensitive PII keys are sent out of context. Our AI summary algorithms comply strictly with enterprise-grade isolation standards.

3. SECURITY IMPLEMENTATION
------------------------------------------------------
We maintain standard electronic controls, local encryption pathways, and role-based interface locks to defend against illegal intrusion. All communication to third-party endpoints is encrypted using high-grade TLS pathways.

4. USER ACTIONS & CONSENTS
------------------------------------------------------
You have complete, absolute control over your operational datasets. At any time, you can:
* Wipe your local databases instantly.
* Export your entire books directory via Excel or JSON formats.
* Update, edit, or remove staff members from the local directory logs.
