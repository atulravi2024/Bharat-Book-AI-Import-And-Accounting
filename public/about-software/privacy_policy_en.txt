🔐 Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority. We believe your financial data should remain strictly under your control.

🏠 Local First Architecture
This application is designed as an offline-first / local-first platform.
  • Where does my data go? Your ledger entries, GST reports, vouchers, and contact masters are stored locally on your device or designated server.
  • We do not surreptitiously upload your financial data to external analytics servers.

🤖 AI Features & External Connections
Certain intelligent features require internet connectivity:
  • AI Voucher Parsing: When you use the "AI Extraction" or "Smart Match" tools, the specific transaction strings are sent securely to the AI provider.
  • What is sent? Only the data you explicitly choose to parse. We do not send your entire ledger to the cloud.

👨‍💻 Administrator Controls
System Administrators have the ability to:
  • Monitor active users and manage group role restrictions.
  • Audit changes to the Ledger and Inventory masters.
  • Wipe or anonymize sandbox data.

📜 Your Rights
As a user, you retain full rights to:
  • Export your data (CSV, JSON, PDF) at any time.
  • Completely delete local browser storage caches.
  • Audit the API calls made through the settings dashboard.

If you have concerns about data handling, please perform an Architectural Integrity check from the Security Settings panel.
