📜 Terms and Conditions (Niyam aur Shartein)

Bharat Book AI mein aapka swagat hai. Is system ko access ya use karne ka matlab hai ki aap in terms se agree karte hain.

1. 👤 Account Responsibilities
  • Security: Apne login credentials ki confidentiality maintain karna aapki zimmedari hai.
  • Accuracy: Aapko ensure karna hoga ki saari financial entries, voucher imports, aur ledger data accurate ho. System AI suggestions deta hai, par final verification aapko khud karna hoga.

2. ⚖️ Compliance aur Law
  • Legal Usage: Aap is platform ko strictly lawful accounting aur business management ke liye use karne par agree karte hain.
  • Tax Regulations: Generated GST reports aur tax summaries ko official submission se pehle ek qualified professional se verify karwana zaroori hai.

3. 🚫 Acceptable Use
  • No System Abuse: Aap system ki integrity ko disrupt karne ya administrative security controls ko bypass karne ki koshish nahi karenge.
  • API Usage: Automated AI requests ko system administrator dwara define ki gayee limits ke andar hi rehna chahiye.

4. 🛑 Termination of Access
  • Agar aap in terms ko violate karte hain ya system ki security ko compromise karte hain, toh System Administrator ke paas aapki access suspend ya terminate karne ka right hai.

5. 🔄 Updates to Terms
  • Nayi operational policies ko reflect karne ke liye in terms ko organization ya system administrators waqt-waqt par update kar sakte hain.
